Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, log expenses, or contact support. This may include:

  • Personal information (name, email address, password)
  • Expense data (amount, category, date, description)
  • Usage information (features used, frequency of use)
  • Device and technical information (IP address, browser type, operating system)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our service
  • Personalize your experience and show relevant content
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance functionality
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices:

  • Expense data is encrypted at rest and in transit
  • We use secure servers with regular security audits
  • Data backups are performed regularly with encryption
  • We retain your data as long as your account remains active
  • Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems

4. Third Party Services

We may share your information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our service:

  • Payment processors (for any premium features)
  • Analytics providers (to improve user experience)
  • Email service providers (for transactional communications)
  • Cloud hosting providers (for data storage and delivery)

We require these parties to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Restrict or object to certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.
